Kimiya Jozani

I am a PhD student in Industrial and Systems Engineering at Virginia Tech. My research develops optimization and machine learning methods for epidemic management, healthcare operations, invasive species control, and decision-making under uncertainty.

Research

Methodology

Stochastic optimization, distributionally robust optimization, CVaR optimization, machine learning, and learning-enhanced decomposition algorithms.

Applications

Epidemic management, healthcare policy, psychiatric operations, invasive species control, and behavioral decision-making.
